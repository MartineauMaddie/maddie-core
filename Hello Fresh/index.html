Black Bean Rice Bowl

Get Started

Preheat the oven to 450℉. Wash and dry all the produce. Start the recipe when the oven is ready.

*Chef's Hack

Toasting spices allows them to open up and release their full flavour potential!

Roast Sweet Potatoes

  • Peel, than cut sweet potatoes into ½-inch pieces.
  • To a parchment-lined baking sheet, add sweet potatoes, half the Chili-Cumin Spice Blend, and 1 tbsp oil (double 4 portions). Season with salt and pepper, then toss to coat.
  • Roast on the middle rack of the oven for 15-18 min, stirring halfway through, until tender and golden-brown.

Cook Rice

  • Meanwhile, to a medium pot, add rice, 1 tbsp butter, and 1 ⅓ cups water (double both for 4 portions) . Season with salt. Bring to a boil over high heat.
  • Once boiling, cover and reduce heat to medium-low. Cook for 12-15 min, until rice is tender and water is absorbed.
  • Set aside for 5 min, off heat and still covered.

Prepare Ingredients

  • Meanwhile, peel, then cut shallot into ¼-inch pieces.
  • Finely chop cilantro.
  • Zest, then juice lime.
  • Drain and rinse beans.
  • Halve tomatoes.

If you've opted to add shrimp, drain and rinse shrimp, then pat dry with paper towels. Season with salt and pepper. In a large non-stick pan, heat 1 tbsp oil (double for 4 portions) over medium-high heat. When the pan is hot, add shrimp. Cook for 2-3 min, stirring occasionally, until shrimp just turn pink.** Transfer shrimp to a bowl, then cover to keep warm. Use the same pan to cook beans in step 4.

Cook beans

  • In a large non-stick pan, heat 2 tsp oil (double for 4 portions) over medium-high heat.
  • When the pan is hot, add half the shallots. Cook for 2-3 min, stirring occasionally, until softened.
  • Add tomato sauce base and remaining Chili-Cumin Spice Blend. Cook for 30 sec, stirring to toast.*
  • Stir in 2 tbsp water (double for 4 portions).
  • Add beans. Cook for 2-3 min, stirring occasionally, until warmed through. Season with salt and pepper, to taste.

Make Salsa & Crema

  • Meanwhile, to a medium bowl, add tomatoes, cilantro, remaining shallots, half the lime juice , and 1 tsp oil (double for 4 portions). Season with salt and pepper, then stir to combine.
  • To a small bowl, add sour cream, half the lime zest, remaining lime juice and 2 tsp water (double for 4 portions). Season with salt then stir to combine.

Finish & Serve

  • To rice, add remaining lime zest. Fluff rice with a fork.
  • Divide rice, sweet potatoes, and beans between bowls.
  • Drizzle lime crema over top, then top with salsa.
  • Enjoy!

Top bowls with shrimp when you plate them.
