Black Bean Rice Bowl

Get Started

Preheat the oven to 450°. Wash and dry all the produce. Start the recipe when the oven is ready.

Roast Sweet Potatoes

  • Peel, than cut sweet potatoes into 1/2-inch pieces.

  • To a parchment-lined baking sheet, add sweet potatoes, one-third of the Chili-Cumin Spice Blend, and 1 tbsp oil (double 4 portions)
    Season with salt and pepper, then toss to coat.

  • Roast on the middle rack of the oven for 15-18 min, stirring halfway through, until tender.

Cook Rice

  • Meanwhile, to a medium pot, add rice, 1 tbsp butter, and 1 1/4 cups water (double both for 4 portions).
    Season with salt. Bring to a boil over high heat.

  • Once boiling, cover and reduce heat to medium-low. Cook for 12-15 min, until rice is tender and water is absorbed.

  • Set aside for 5 min, off heat and stir until covered.

Prepare Ingredients

  • Meanwhile, peel, then cut shallot into 1/4-inch pieces.

  • Finely chop cilantro.

  • Zest, then juice lime.

  • Drain and rinse beans.

  • Halve tomatoes.

Cook beans

  • In a large non-stick pan, heat 2 tsp oil (double for 4 portions) over medium-high heat.

  • When the pan is hot, add half the shallots. Cook for 2-3 min, stirring occasionally, until softened.

  • Add tomato sauce base and remaining Chili-Cumin Spice Blend Cook for 30 sec, stirring to toast.*

  • Stir in 2 tbsp water (double for 4 portions).

  • Add beans .Cook for 2-3 min, stirring occasionally, until warmed through. Season with salt and pepper, to taste.
    If you've opted to add beef, add beef to the pan when you add shalots. Cook for 3-5 min, breaking up beef into smaller pieces, until no pink remain.** Carefully drain and discard excess fat, then continue with the recipe.

Make Salsa & Crema

  • Meanwhile, to a medium bowl, add tomatoes, cilantro, remaining shallots, half the lime juice, and 1 tsp oil (double for 4 portions).
    Season with salt and pepper, then stir to combine.

  • To a small bowl, add sour cream, half the lime zest, and 1 tbsp water (double for 4 portions). Season with salt then stir to combine.

Finish & Serve

  • To rice, add remaining lime zest and remaining lime juice. Fluff rice with a fork.

  • Divide rice, sweet potatoes, and beans between bowls.

  • Drizzle crema over top, then top with salsa and cheese.

  • Enjoy!
